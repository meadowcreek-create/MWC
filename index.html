How it works

Set it up once. Enjoy it forever.

1

A family member sets up the album

Enter a PIN to unlock setup mode. Add each family member — grandparents at the top, then parents, then the home person, then children and grandchildren below. Tap to add their photo and name.

2

The album opens to the home person

When a senior opens the app, they see their own photo front and center. One button returns them here no matter where they are in the album.

3

Swipe to move through the family

Swipe up to see parents and grandparents. Swipe down to see children and grandchildren. Swipe left or right to see siblings. It works with a slow drag — no quick flick needed.

4

Works completely offline

No Wi-Fi needed, ever. All photos and names are stored privately on the phone. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
