How it works

Set it up once. Enjoy it anytime.

1

Someone sets up the album

Enter a PIN to unlock setup mode. Add each family member across up to seven generations — great-grandparents, grandparents, parents, the home person and their siblings, children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren. Tap each frame to add a photo and name.

2

The album opens to the person it was made for

Every time the app opens, it goes straight to the person the album was built around — their photo, front and center. One button always brings them back here.

3

Navigate with swipes or on-screen buttons

Swipe or tap the navigation buttons to move through the family. Up to older generations, down to younger ones, left and right for siblings. Slow and deliberate — no rushing needed.

4

Works completely offline

No Wi-Fi ever needed. All photos are stored privately on the device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
